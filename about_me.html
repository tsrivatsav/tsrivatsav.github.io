Data Scientist at Deloitte

I currently implement generative AI solutions as a data scientist at Deloitte for clients such as the National Institute of Health. My solutions typically involve developing RAG systems using large language models and vector databases, and I deliver these solutions in the form of scalable and interactive web applications. I am especially well-versed in creating data pipelines to train and deploy machine learning models on cloud platforms. I enjoy working with all forms of data and believe in the deliberate process of making observations and then finding patterns in those observations to better understand our world.

How I Got Started

From an early age I’ve enjoyed playing games – whether it’s videogames, board games, cards, or sports. Although I didn’t realize it until later, games are often just math problems in disguise. There are a set of rules (constraints), objectives (rewards), and moves (actions) over which one needs to optimize for. Naturally, I was inclined towards math when I studied in school and sought ways to apply its abstract ideas.

Top Rated+ Machine Learning Freelancer on Upwork

My freelance experience involved building AI products from scratch for clients that I met via Upwork. I learned how to communicate requirements and performance tradeoffs with non-technical entrepreneurs and had to manage project timelines independently. Software Engineer at Scale AI

I contributed to a variety of high impact projects as a software engineer at Scale AI – the most notable of which were OpenAI’s GPT-4o and Google Gemini Code Assist. As part of these client projects I conducted reinforcement learning through human feedback (RLHF) specifically for code generation in programming languages such as Python, C++, and Java. I evaluated and modified model responses based on correctness, conciseness, helpfulness, and safety. I reviewed others’ evaluations and created benchmarks to improve the quality of our taskers’ data labelling. The dynamic environment of a rapidly growing company provided invaluable learning experiences as our team demonstrated adaptability and resilience every day.
